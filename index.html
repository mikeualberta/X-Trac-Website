Manufacturing

OEM-Grade Precision

Most aftermarket suppliers switch factories to chase the lowest price, sacrificing consistency and durability. X-TRAC is manufactured to OEM specifications through a controlled, fully integrated production process at dedicated production sites, ensuring repeatable quality and reliable field performance.

Each track is built as an integrated system, combining metal cores, jointless steel cord reinforcement, and advanced rubber compounds engineered for durability and long service life.

01

Advanced Rubber Compound

Four-stage blending of natural and synthetic rubber with black carbon additives delivers strength without brittleness. High resistance to abrasion, tearing, and fatigue. Anti-piercing and anti-aging additives prevent premature cracking and UV degradation.

02

Metal Core Construction

Chemically treated, forged cast-iron lugs with dual-coat protection ensure a clean, rust-free bonding surface. Controlled vulcanization creates a permanent rubber-to-metal bond that virtually eliminates lug pop-outs, even under extreme operating loads.

03

Jointless Steel Cord Reinforcement

Continuous, high-tensile steel cable wound and sealed within a rubber membrane. No joints. No weak points. Individually wrapped cords reduce internal friction and distribute load uniformly across the full track width.
